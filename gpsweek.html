About the Event

GPS Day is a special occasion to remember the day our organization was formed. It celebrates our journey, achievements, and the people who made it possible. Every year, we come together to reflect on our past, celebrate the present, and look forward to a brighter future.

Event Highlights

  • Cultural performances
  • Guest speeches and honors
  • Team awards and recognitions
  • Fun activities and games
  • Community dinner

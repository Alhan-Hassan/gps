The History of God's Prayer Squad (GPS): A Journey of Faith and Prayer

Origins: The Spark of Divine Guidance (Early 2000s)

GPS began as a small prayer group in the early 2000s, inspired by analogies comparing God's direction to a Global Positioning System. Prayer serves as "God's Positioning System," helping individuals navigate life's uncertainties with faith. The group was initially formed by a few dedicated individuals in local churches, emphasizing collective prayer for personal and communal breakthroughs. By 2010, it had grown into informal meetings where members shared testimonies of answered prayers, drawing from biblical teachings like Isaiah 41:10: "So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God."

This period laid the foundation for GPS as a "squad" united in spiritual warfare through prayer.

Expansion and Digital Presence (2015‚Äì2020)

In 2015, GPS embraced technology, launching social media accounts to connect prayer partners worldwide. The Instagram account @god_s_prayer_squad_gps became a hub for daily prayer prompts, Bible verses, and virtual prayer sessions. This digital shift was influenced by the rise of online faith communities, where prayer was seen as a "GPS for Life's Purpose & Guidance," as highlighted in motivational videos and devotionals.

During this era, GPS organized prayer chains for global events, such as natural disasters and pandemics, emphasizing verses like Ezra 8:23: "So we fasted and petitioned our God about this, and he answered our prayer." By 2020, the squad had thousands of followers, with prayer requests submitted via DMs and live sessions fostering a sense of unity.

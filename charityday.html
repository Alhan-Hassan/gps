The Story of Our Charity Day

On this special day, we came together as God's Prayer Squad to make a tangible difference in our community. We believe that prayer, when combined with action, can create miracles. Our annual Charity Day is a testament to this belief, bringing us closer to those in need and allowing us to be the hands and feet of our faith.

This year, our focus was on providing essential resources to homeless families in the city center. We organized a food drive, a clothing collection, and a mobile clinic offering basic health check-ups. The outpouring of support from volunteers and donors was truly heartwarming and a powerful display of community spirit.

Our Impact: A Glimpse of the Day

Food Donations

"GPS provides various food donations for those in need, where some of the food is cooked and shared together throughout the day, starting with tea and continuing to lunch."

Clothing & Warmth

"GPS also contributes clothing for those in need to enable them to have a better appearance. GPS provides different types of clothes in various sizes as well as shoes."

Health & Wellness

"In the area of Health and Wellness, GPS also supports beneficiaries by providing essential body-cleaning items and tools such as soap, toothpaste, toothbrushes, and other hygiene products. These efforts help promote personal cleanliness, prevent diseases, and improve overall well-being."
